About Us

Maharashtra State Electricity Distribution Company Limited is the Government of Maharashtra’s primary electricity distribution utility, delivering reliable power to over 3.5 crore consumers across the state.Established in 2005 following the restructuring of the Maharashtra State Electricity Board, MSEDCL operates one of India’s largest distribution networks, supporting households, industries, businesses, and agriculture. With a power portfolio of over 33,000 MW, including a growing share of renewable energy, the organisation is focused on ensuring a reliable, affordable, and sustainable electricity supply. Through ongoing investments in digital systems, smart metering, and infrastructure modernisation, MSEDCL continues to enhance efficiency, strengthen service delivery, and support Maharashtra’s energy future.
